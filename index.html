USER GUIDE
↓ FULL MANUAL (PDF) ↓ QUICK HOW-TO (PDF)

1 · What this is

Kunoch Command is your family-office command center. You issue a directive; Fredrick (Chief of Staff) selects the right specialists from a roster of seven — CFO, Legal, M&A, PPP, Board, Tech, Research — has each analyse it, then writes one decisive executive briefing. Everything is scoped to the specific business you're working in.

2 · First-time setup

The app works immediately in local mode (paste your Anthropic key in the sidebar API panel). To sync across phone + desktop and enable meetings and auto-reviews, sign in:

  1. Set the server key — in Supabase → Project Settings → Edge Functions → Secrets, add ANTHROPIC_API_KEY = your sk-ant-… key.
  2. Allow the login redirect — Supabase → Authentication → URL Configuration → Site URL and Redirect URLs = https://kelechidozie-claude.github.io/kunoch-command/ (add the /** wildcard too).
  3. Sign in — sidebar → SYNC panel → your email → SEND MAGIC LINK → click the link. Repeat on your phone with the same email to share one dataset.

3 · Issuing a directive

  1. Pick the active business (top-left switcher).
  2. Optionally drop documents (PDF, Word, Excel, images…) for Fredrick to analyse.
  3. Choose Scope: Single business or Cross-business.
  4. Choose Agent mode (sidebar): AUTO (Fredrick picks the agents) or MANUAL (you tick the agents).
  5. Type your directive and press ISSUE (or ⌘/Ctrl + Enter).

Each specialist card shows Fredrick's brief and the analysis; the gold card at the bottom is the executive briefing. Use EXPORT PDF for a letterhead briefing, or COPY on any card.

4 · Follow-ups

After a run, use CONTINUE THE THREAD under the briefing. Fredrick routes your follow-up to only the relevant specialist(s), builds on the prior conclusions, and stores the exchange inside that run.

5 · Cross-business directives

Set Scope to Cross-business, tick 2+ businesses, and issue. Fredrick briefs the agents on synergies, conflicts, resource contention and sequencing across those businesses. The result is filed under every business involved and badged ◆ N BIZ in history.

6 · Businesses & profiles

Use + NEW BUSINESS or ⚙ EDIT CURRENT. The profile (positioning, stage, priorities, non-negotiables, brand spec) is injected into every directive for that business, plus the last few decisions as memory. The Scheduled Auto-Review toggle lives here.

7 · Meetings (MEETINGS tab)

Sign in, then drop a transcript or notes (.vtt/.srt/.txt/Word/PDF/Excel/images). The original is stored securely with a SHA-256 fingerprint and provenance in the audit log; Fredrick normalises it into a structured record (decisions, action items with owner & due, open questions, flags). WEEKLY REVIEW summarises the last 30 days and carries forward open items. Voice audio (mp3/m4a/wav/mp4…) is auto-transcribed with speaker diarisation — set a DEEPGRAM_API_KEY secret on the server to enable it.

8 · Scheduled auto-reviews

Tick Scheduled Auto-Review on any business. Every Monday 07:00 Africa/Lagos a weekly portfolio review is generated from that business's profile + recent meetings and filed into its run history automatically. Tick Also email me to have each review delivered to your inbox (needs a RESEND_API_KEY secret and a verified sender address set on the server). Requires the Anthropic server key.

9 · Sync, cost & backup

Sync: signed in = data lives in the cloud and every device shares it; signed out = local only. Cost meter (top bar) shows this interaction's spend and month-to-date. Export / Import (sidebar) backs up businesses + history to a JSON file — never your key.

10 · Troubleshooting

  • Magic link goes to 127.0.0.1 / localhost — send the link from the live site (not a local copy), delete old link emails, and confirm the Redirect URLs are saved in Supabase.
  • "Sign-in rate limit exceeded" — the email sender is throttled; connect your Resend SMTP (Authentication → SMTP) and raise the email rate limit.
  • AI call fails when signed in — the ANTHROPIC_API_KEY secret isn't set in Supabase.
  • Rate-limited mid-run — the app retries automatically; wait ~30s and RETRY the card, or raise your Anthropic tier.
